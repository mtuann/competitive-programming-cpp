# ICPC Playbook

## What Matters Most

- breadth
- speed of recognition
- team communication and notebook retrieval

## Training Rhythm

- mixed rounds
- notebook drills
- pair or team review when possible

## Before A Contest

- skim build-kit pages and notebook sheets
- make sure template retrieval is immediate
- agree on team workflow for debugging and ownership

## After A Contest

- classify misses by topic and by team process
- add only high-value notebook entries, not every detail
